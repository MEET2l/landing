FAQ

This program is conducted fully online, allowing children to participate from home through simple digital platforms like WhatsApp or Zoom. Parents can enroll their child by completing the registration form and selecting a preferred batch. After registration, you receive guidance, schedules, and activity instructions directly on your mobile device.

The program includes age-appropriate activities such as early learning games, creative art tasks, language-building exercises, motor skill development, storytelling, and interactive worksheets. All activities are designed by ECE experts to enhance curiosity, growth, and foundational skills. Parents receive step-by-step instructions for completing activities comfortably at home with their children.

Yes, Parental supervision is recommended to help children understand instructions, participate confidently, and stay engaged during activities. Parents also provide emotional support and help with materials. Supervision ensures better learning outcomes, stronger bonding, and smooth completion of tasks. However, the activities are simple and require minimal effort from the parent’s side.

You will receive regular updates through activity submissions, feedback messages, observation notes, and progress tracking. Trainers review your child’s work and provide personalized suggestions for improvement. Visible changes in your child’s communication, creativity, confidence, and daily learning habits will also clearly show the program’s positive impact over time.

Unlike traditional Anganwadi or ICDS programs, this initiative offers structured, personalized, and research-based learning activities tailored specifically for each child. Parents receive continuous support through digital guidance, recorded sessions, and expert monitoring. The program focuses on holistic development, early learning milestones, and family engagement, making it more impactful and modern.

The program lasts for three months and includes weekly sessions designed to match the child’s pace. Each session focuses on developmental learning, skill-building, and hands-on activities. Parents receive guidance throughout the week, making it easy to continue the learning process daily without pressure or strict time commitments.

If parents miss a session, they can still complete the activities using recorded videos, instructions, and shared resources. All materials remain accessible anytime, allowing families to continue learning at a convenient pace. This flexibility ensures that children do not miss developmental progress even when parents are busy or unavailable.

After program completion, children receive follow-up activities, growth guidance, and optional advanced learning modules. Trainers may provide additional suggestions based on developmental needs. Parents can continue using shared resources to support daily learning. This extended support helps children maintain progress and smoothly transition into formal schooling environments.
